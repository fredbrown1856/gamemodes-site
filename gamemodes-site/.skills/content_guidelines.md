# 📝 Content Guidelines

## Proprietary Information — DO NOT INCLUDE

The following must **never** appear in any public-facing content (website, README, DEVLOG, etc.):

### NPC Engine Basis
- **NO references to "IFS"** or "Internal Family Systems" (the therapeutic framework by Richard Schwartz)
- **NO IFS-specific terminology** such as "parts", "Self", "managers", "firefighters", "exiles", "active_part", "Self-energy", "unblending", etc.
- The NPC engine's psychological modeling is described generically as:
  - "Proprietary psychological modeling layer"
  - "Psychologically grounded dialogue"
  - "Structured psychological profiles"
  - "3 Psychological Modes (protective, reactive, vulnerable)"

### Acceptable Generic Terms
- ✅ "psychological profiles" / "NPC profiles"
- ✅ "psychological modeling layer"
- ✅ "protective mode", "reactive mode", "vulnerable mode"
- ✅ "stress tracking", "relationship modeling", "emotional states"
- ✅ "per-faction psychological templates"
- ✅ "NPC dialogue engine"

### Unacceptable IFS-Specific Terms
- ❌ "IFS" / "Internal Family Systems"
- ❌ "parts work" / "parts therapy"
- ❌ "active_part", "manager", "firefighter", "exile"
- ❌ "Self-leadership", "Self-energy"
- ❌ "blending" / "unblending"

## If You Find a Violation
If you encounter any IFS or proprietary terminology in public files, flag it immediately and replace with acceptable generic alternatives.
